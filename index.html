Di Website Nyam Banana

Nyam Banana adalah sebuah restoran yang didirikan pada tahun 2020. Restoran ini menyediakan menu makanan yang manis dengan berbagai varian rasa.

Various kinds of flavors and various types of preparations are available at this restaurant, such as banana nuggets, banana roll, etc. Starting from an effort to fill spare time during a pandemic, Nyam Banana is now one of the most popular restaurants for many people.
